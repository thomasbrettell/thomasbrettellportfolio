The Carbon Quiz
DESIGNING IN THE ANTHROPOCENE • FIRST PERSON INFORMATION GAME DEVELOPMENT

First person game which aims to tackle misinformation surrounding climate change, specifically carbon emissions. The game was made in Unity and requires the player to complete a Todo List in the most carbon friendly way.
This project recieved the highest mark out of my graduating cohort.

Sleep Orbit
DESIGNING FOR SPACE TRAVEL • MENTAL WELLBEING UI APP DESIGN

Design for a mobile app and wearable ring that monitors sleep to assist with the difficulties of sleeping while residing in space. Additional features such as stats, mindfulness, music and helpful tips assist travellers with overcoming the prevelent psychological harm that they may face while travelling in space.

Global Causes of Death
WEB BASED DATA VISUALISATION • EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS

Web based data visualisation which allows the user to select any two countries and see a break down of how their populus died from any year between 1990 and 2017.

Success at the summer Olympics became more globalised as a result of World War 2
STATIC INFORMATION VISUALISATION

Information visualisation which shows the total medal count and percentage medal count of every country both before and after World War 2. This data shows that after the fallout of World War 2, the international community became more globalised.

Is Summer the Season for Domestic Violence?
STATIC INFORMATION VISUALISATION • EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS

Information visualisation which shows the percentage of domestic violence cases that occured in NSW from 1995 to 2019 and shows that Summer is the most prevelent season for domestic violence.
